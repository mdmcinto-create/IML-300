Spotlight

Featuring emerging Black artists and creators

Myracle

Film Director and Poet

Myracle is an inspiration to many rising creatives, specializing in short film storytelling, poetry, and has an impressive collection of projects that include music videos, and a commercial for DoorDash. See more of her work at her Instagram handle @majormyra

SloMo

Fashion Model, Musician, and Film creative

Slomo is a multihyphenate artist that specializes in music and acting. He has collaborated with Shaboozy on their new record: 100 Acres Woods. See more of his work on his Instagram at @slomogoku

Samba

Filmmaker

Samba is a highly talented colorist and filmmaker that specializes in short film storytelling. His motto "with purpose" truly represents the passion and behind his work. See his films on Instagram at @sambafilms

DonYé Taylor

Film Creative Director

DonYé explores visual storytelling through creative direction in film. She is also the founder of 'nüclei' a brand that sells artistic home good pieces made from repurposed materials. Her Instagram can be found at @donyetaylor

Nathaniel Richards

Poet and Filmmaker

Nathaniel is Published poet, and award-winning Documentary Filmmaker, telling stories from underrepresented local communities. See his work on Instagram at @naeuk
