Who We Are

The Black Entertainment Collective (BEC) is a dynamic community dedicated to uniting Black screenwriters, directors, and creatives working in film and television. We believe in the power of storytelling to shape culture, challenge perspectives, and create lasting change in the entertainment industry.

Founded on the principle that representation matters both in front of and behind the camera, BEC serves as a platform for emerging and established Black creatives to connect, collaborate, and amplify their voices in an industry that has historically marginalized them.

Our Mission

To elevate and celebrate Black voices in entertainment by creating spaces for connection, highlighting groundbreaking work through our publications, and fostering a community where Black creatives can thrive, collaborate, and shape the future of film and television.

What We Do

The BEC provides a multifaceted platform for Black creatives in entertainment, featuring emerging talent, publishing important narratives and industry insights, and creating connections that strengthen our collective voice.

Our Vision

We envision an entertainment industry where Black creatives are not just present but celebrated, where our stories are told authentically, and where the next generation of Black filmmakers, writers, and directors have clear pathways to success.

Through our work, we aim to shift the narrative, challenge stereotypes, and ensure that Black excellence in entertainment is recognized, supported, and amplified at every level of the industry.
